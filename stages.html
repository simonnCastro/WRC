WRC Stage Types

Explore three real rally surfaces and how they change the racing action.

Tarmac – Monte Carlo

Monte Carlo is famous for changing grip, icy corners, and narrow mountain roads. Drivers must switch between dry, wet, and snow setups unexpectedly.

Gravel – Rally Finland

Known as the “Grand Prix of Rallying,” Finland features super-fast gravel roads, crests, and massive jumps. Mastering weight transfer is key.

Snow – Rally Sweden

Snowbanks and studded tires define this winter rally. Drivers lean on the banks for high-speed cornering.
